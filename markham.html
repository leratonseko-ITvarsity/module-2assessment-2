MARKHAM

Markham was established in 1873 in Parow, a suburb north of Cape Town and they form part of The Foschini Group. They are the largest stand alone men's fashion retail chain in South Africa and have in excess of 250 stores throughout the country in shopping centres in major metropolitan areas as well as other towns.

They offer a broad spectrum of available and trendy fashion for men including: jeans, smart wear, accessories, footwear and gadgets. Their philosophy is for their clients to “feel great, look good and sharer their passion for style.” Their stores are one-stop shops with their clothing ranges catering for all occasions. Their most prominent ranges include “Relay,” their casual,fun and trendy range and “Cignal,” their more formal and sophisticated clothing range.Their target market spans almost all ages with their most prominent age group being 19-25 year olds.
